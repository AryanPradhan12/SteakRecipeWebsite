What is a Panozzo?

The Panozzo is a famous Italian dish that was found in Bolgna long time back. It is known as the mixture of a pizza and a sandwich; using pizza dough as the bread for a sandwich and pizza sauce. This recipe takes the famous Panozzo dish from Italian culture and fuses it with a Sirloin steak cut.

Ingredients

1.)Sourdough bread - This ingredient brings vital cultural essence to the recipe as it creates the hard Italian styled bread for the sandwich.

2.)Mozzarella cheese - This ingredient also reflects the taste of a famous Italian dish (pizza).

3.)Ribeye steak - This beef cut is best suggested for the dish as a ribeye steak really complements the Italian pizza flavor.

4.)Lettuce - It is important to have greens in your sandwich and so lettuce is the most common green to add in a panozzo.

5.)Pizza sauce - The most important ingredient for the Italian flavor.

6.)Pepper and salt - These two ingredients are very important for marinating the steak before cooking it.

7.)Butter - Cooking your steak in butter is the best way to bring out the soft and tenderness in the steak.

Steps to cook

1.)Take your sourdough bread and cut it into half.

2.)Now take your pizza sauce and spread it on both sides of the sourdough bread.

3.) Rub pepper and salt into your steak, then cook the ribeye steak onto a pan to your liking (rare, medium rare, well done, etc.).

4.) Place the ribeye steak onto the bottom bread, add a little pizza sauce, then the lettuce, and then mozzarella.

5.) Place the sandwich onto the pan with butter and cook it until the cheese melts.
